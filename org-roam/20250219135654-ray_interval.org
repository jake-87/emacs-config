#+title:Ray, Interval

A "Ray" in [[id:0C7F6400-D50D-4ADC-B130-B3AA9EF28415][Interval notation]] is something with an open infinity bound on one side. The following are all rays:

- $(1, \infty)$

- $[0, \infty]$

- $(-\infty, \infty)$
