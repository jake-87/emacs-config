
#+title:Matrix, algebra

* Definition:
A matrix is some rectangular collection of elements, eg:

\begin{align*}
&\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix}
\\\\
&\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
