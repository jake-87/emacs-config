#+title:Reduced row echelon form, algebra


* Definition

A matrix that is in [[id:6ED51834-6053-45E0-AB13-282C70B00A90][Row echelon form]], and:
- No row is all-zeros
- Every leading entry is 1, and the only nonzero entry in its row
