#+title:Antisymmetric, relation

* Definition

A binary relation $R$ is antisymmetric if $\forall x\, y, R(x,y) \land R(y,x) \implies x = y$.

For example, less than or equal is antisymmetric: $x \leq y \land x \leq y \implies x = y$.
